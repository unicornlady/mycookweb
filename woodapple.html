Wood Apple Juice

Ingredients:

1 ripe wood apple (also known as bael fruit)


Water

Sugar or jaggery (optional, for sweetness)

Instructions:
1. Break open the wood apple and scoop out the pulp.
2. Put the pulp in a bowl and add some water. Mash and mix it well to extract the juice.
3. Strain the mixture through a sieve or cheesecloth to separate the juice from the seeds and fibers.
4. If desired, add sugar or jaggery to sweeten the juice.
5. Chill the juice in the refrigerator or serve it over ice.

Enjoy your wood apple juice!
